Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions.

What is the difference between a Dentist and an Orthodontist?
Dentists deal with a wide variety of oral health problems while orthodontists specialize in alignment of teeth and jaws.
Can dentists treat orthodontic conditions?
Yes. Some dentists provide orthodontic treatments and guidance depending on their qualifications and expertise.
How often should I visit a dentist?
Generally every 3 to 6 months or twice a year for routine checkups.
Do you provide Invisible Braces?
Yes. We provide Invisible Braces and advanced orthodontic treatment options.
Do you provide Hair Transplant treatments?
Yes. Hair Restoration, PRP Therapy and FUE Hair Transplant procedures are available.
